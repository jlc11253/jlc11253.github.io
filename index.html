FAQs

Is there parking at the venue?
There is very limited paid parking right outside, but it is right in the middle of town so you could easily walk from the station car park, John Lewis or St David's car parks.


What if I have dietary requirements?
The food will be buffet style and we will have covered all of the usual suspects (vegan, veggie etc) but if you have anything specific you think we should know about drop us a message asap and we'll pass it on to the caterers.


What about your ceremony?
We are doing a very small ceremony, with our main celebration being this evening party. Don't worry, Joe is still threatening to do a speech.

We are quite tight on numbers, so the invite is for whoever we addressed it to when we sent it.
